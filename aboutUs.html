Our Company

Kai Ko Shipping Company Limited is a seafarer recruitment and placement services company established in 2014. We connect our clients to shipping companies from Singapore, Malaysia and around South East Asia. Types of merchant ships for our services include General Cargo, Container and Bulk Carrier. We transfer qualified seafarers who meet the given criteria for the employment on the ship. We also provide training services for respective ranks and positions.
